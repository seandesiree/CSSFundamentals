Chocolate Chip Cookie Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup brown sugar, packed
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a large bowl , cream together the butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until smooth. Beat in the eggs, one at a time, then stir in the vanilla extract.
  3. In a separate bowl, combine the flour, baking soda, and salt. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients and mix well. Stir in the chocolate chips.
  4. Drop rounded tablespoons of dough onto ungreased baking sheets.
  5. Bake for 8 to 10 minutes, or until lightly golden brown. Allow cookies to cool on baking sheets for 5 minutes before transferring to wire racks to cool completely.
